Delete Your Nutrea Account

If you no longer wish to use Nutrea, you can permanently delete your account from the app. This removes your profile and nutrition data from our production systems.

How to delete your account (in the app)

  1. Open the Nutrea app and go to Profile.
  2. Tap Settings → Account.
  3. Choose Delete Account.
  4. Confirm your choice in the pop-up. You may be asked to type DELETE for security.

⚠️ This action is permanent. Once deleted, your account and data cannot be recovered.

What will be deleted

  • Your profile (name, email, preferences)
  • Nutrition logs, photos, goals and progress
  • In-app measurements and insights

We may retain minimal records required by law (e.g., billing logs) for the statutory period.

After you delete

  • You will be logged out on all devices.
  • Deletion is initiated immediately and typically completes within minutes; full removal from backups may take up to 30 days due to safe disaster-recovery practices.
  • If you signed up via Apple/Google, revoke access in your Apple/Google account settings.

If you have any questions or need assistance, please contact us at contact@foodways.be.
